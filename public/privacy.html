Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2025

WorthIt is a Chrome extension designed to help Indian shoppers make smarter purchasing decisions. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

WorthIt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or user activity. All features, including cost-per-use calculations and time-to-afford metrics, operate entirely on your device.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, and we do not use analytics or external tracking services.

Permissions

Our extension uses Chrome permissions (such as activeTab) solely to display insights on the page you're viewing. These are required for the extension to function but do not involve data collection.

Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change, we will update this page accordingly.
